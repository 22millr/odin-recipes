Coffee

Best black coffee for your early mornings.

We all know coffee is the most popular choices to drink in the mornings, so we made the best coffee recipe to make at the start of your day. This recipe is not only easy to make, it also tastes great and will keep you energized throughout your busy schedule.

Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 1 fluid ounce hot water
  • 3/4 cup coffee
  • 1 fluid coffee-flavored liquer
  • 1 tbsp whipped cream

Steps

  1. Pour sugar and hot water into a coffee mug.
  2. Stir in the coffee and liqueur.
  3. then spoon whipped cream gently on top of the coffee.
